Welcome to Dungeon Crawler!

You are going to play against snakes (green) and rats (red) in a maze. Move your player (yellow) with the arrow keys.
If you move into a snake or a rat, you make an attack and you might either loose health or hurt the enemy.

The game will end eighter when you kill all the enemies, when you die or when you press the Escape button.

Press Enter to start the game.

Good luck and have fun!
